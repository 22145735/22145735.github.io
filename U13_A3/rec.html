Recommended Phones

A midrange and a flagship phone are 2 completely different types of phone sold to the phone market. Your iPhone, or your Samsung phones, depending on how recent and how expensive it is, are flagship phones. Flagship phones are high-end phones that shows off the best performance, and features that are built into the phone, but extremely expensive. For example, your iPhone 17 or your Samsung Galaxy S25 are both flagship phones. A Mid-range phone on the other hand, are cheap budget smartphones that includes balanced performance, but not top-of-the-line performance like with the flagship phones. They provide efficient and quick use for phones, but more prone to lag after approximately 3 years after buying a mid-range phone.
